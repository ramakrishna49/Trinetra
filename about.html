Tools & technology

Measurement-led inspections, not quick walk-throughs.

Fresh paint can hide a lot. Our engineers rely on tools and readings – not just visual checks – to uncover seepage, slope issues and electrical risks early.

  • FLIR thermal cameras for hidden dampness and hot spots.
  • Digital moisture meters to assess seepage behind walls and tiles.
  • Laser levels for slope and alignment in bathrooms, balconies & terraces.
  • Load testers & multimeters for DB and socket safety checks.
  • Tile tapping tools to detect hollow / loose tiles without damage.
